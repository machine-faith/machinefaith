Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

1. Information We Collect

We primarily collect the basic business information you provide when you contact us (such as your name, email, phone number, and business details) so that we can communicate with you and build your website.

2. How We Protect Your Data

Security is a core part of how Machine Faith operates. Rather than using outdated platforms like WordPress that are frequently targeted by hackers, we host our websites on the Vercel Edge Network.

  • No Vulnerable Databases: Our websites are "serverless." This means there is no central database of plugins or admin panels for hackers to compromise.
  • Top-Tier Encryption: Every website we manage is automatically secured with bank-grade SSL encryption, meaning any data sent through contact forms is scrambled and protected.
  • Enterprise Security: By using Vercel, your website sits behind the exact same global security shield and spam/bot protection used by massive international companies.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business information to third parties. We only share information with trusted infrastructure providers (like Vercel for hosting, and GitHub for secure code storage) strictly for the purpose of operating your website.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about how your data is handled on your new website, please contact us at Machine Faith's email.
